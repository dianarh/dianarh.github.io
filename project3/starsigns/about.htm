STAR SIGNS

AKA SUN SIGNS

The zodiac is a yearly cycle of 12 different constellations that are equally divided into 12 zones of the sun's apparent path across the heavens. Each different sign of the zodiac has corresponding personality traits, denoted through signs. The signs are: Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces. The signs of the zodiac are separated by their own characteristics into four categories: Air, Fire, Earth, and Water. The characteristics of these four quadrants depict different traits for a person of each sign. These traits include personality, relationships and friendships.
