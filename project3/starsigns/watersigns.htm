WATER SIGNS

CANCER / PISCES / SCORPIO

Water signs are always emotionally reserved, no matter what impression they might give on the surface. Still water, it as been said, runs deep, and the mystery about water pertains to this group (especially Scorpio). A water sign will always become quiet or withdrawn when they are trying to sort things out. It will take some time to get to know them well, for they reveal themselves slowly. They realize their intense emotions hold tremendous power, and they tend to handle their feelings carefully. This element rules the soul. Water does not like to be confined and they will assert their strong need for freedom, no matter how gently it is expressed. When you share a problem with a water sign, they take on your problem as if it were their own. Their ability to sympathize and empathize is awesome; you'll get their full attention, a shoulder to cry on, and some excellent advice to boot.

Water signs are the most sensitive signs of the zodiac. Their emotions guide them in whatever they do. They have strong emotional bonds with other people and empathy towards other people. They don't always make decisions based on the logical thing to do. Rather, they use their emotions as a guide to living. Those who are water signs tend to fall into the artistic realm of the zodiac. Their observations on the human condition often inspire masterpieces of painting, music, photography, literature, or dance. They can be painters or writers and are often very in touch with the beauty of the world. They like to make others happy, even if that means giving up something of them to do so. Water signs get themselves in to trouble with their emotions. They sometimes let others take advantage of their kindness. When this happens, they might retreat into a non-reality based world and seclude themselves from others.
